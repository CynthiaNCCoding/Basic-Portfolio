About Me

I like wearing red and smiling. My current hobbies include riding my motorcycle (not pictured) and exploring North Carolina as a recent transplant from Los Angeles.

Professionally, I have worked in visual effects for feature films. Such projects include the Oscar winning "Curious Case of Benjamin Button" to "50 Shades of Grey" (you'll have to ask me what was computer generated...). Most recently, that love for art and technology has led me to web development where I aim to make the web both beautiful and functional.
